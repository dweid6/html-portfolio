The Best Movies According to Drew

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Deadpool

Starring Ryan Reynolds.
Action-Comedy with detailed backstory and genuine, adult comedy sprinkled in throughout.

Draft Day

Starring Kevin Costner.
Sports movie following multiple story lines throughout the 24 hours surrounding the first round of the NFL Draft.

Forrest Gump

Starring Tom Hanks.
Drama that follows the life of a mentally disabled Alabam man and takes you through real life events in American history from his point of view.
